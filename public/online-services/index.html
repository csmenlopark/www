Online Services

If you can’t join us in person, you can still attend our services via Zoom.

Sunday 10:00 am and Wednesday 7:30 pm

There are two ways to join the Zoom meeting:

  • You may join from your computer or smartphone:
    1. Click this link: Join Meeting
      (Note: If you don’t already have the Zoom application installed, clicking the link will take you to instructions for installing it.)
    2. If prompted, enter this meeting ID: 174873568
    3. If prompted, enter this password: 075187
  • You may join via a regular telephone call:
    1. Dial (669) 900-9128
    2. When prompted, enter this meeting ID: 174873568
    3. When prompted, enter this password: 075187
    4. If prompted for a participant ID, dial # to skip.

The meeting will open approximately 10 minutes before the service. Participants will be automatically muted during the service.

For testimonies on Wednesday, if you wish to share, please use the Raise Hand feature and wait for the usher to call on you.

Instructions for raising your hand
  • Mac or Windows: Click on Reactions, then click on Raise Hand.
  • Older Zoom on Windows: Click on Participants, then in the panel that opens click on Raise Hand.
  • iOS and Android: Tap More, then tap Raise Hand.
  • Telephone: Dial *9.
