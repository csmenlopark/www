<h1 style="text-align: center; margin-top: 4rem; margin-bottom: 3rem;">Healing With Joy: Discovering the Power of Love</h1>

***Healing in Christian Science is joyful**—it’s the natural result of understanding
God as divine Life and Love. Ann Kneeland, a Christian Science practitioner
and lecturer, shares real-life examples of spiritual healing. These examples
show how fear, illness, and polarization dissolve as we discover God’s pure
and spiritual creation. We can all experience joy and freedom through learning
about God’s loving presence and power.*

This talk is sponsored jointly by First Church of Christ, Scientist, San Mateo and First Church of Christ, Scientist, Menlo Park. For more information, see <a href=http://www.cschurchsanmateo.org/lecture-2/ rel=external>First Church of Christ, Scientist, San Mateo's website</a>.
