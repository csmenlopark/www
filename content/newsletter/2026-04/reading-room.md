# April 2026 Newsletter » Reading Rooms Inspire Our Service

*(continued from the newsletter)*

"millions of unprejudiced minds—simple seekers for Truth...." —*Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures*, Mary Baker Eddy, p. 570:14–16

Your Reading Room offers two scheduled activities: a shared Bible Lesson reading on Mondays and a replay of a Christian Science talk on Thursdays. Come and join in!  Our Reading Room at 1127 Chestnut Street is open to the public Monday–Thursday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.

Similar to the healing of Rabbi Louis’s father, which began at another Reading Room, you can learn more about our daily practice of Christian Science healing as taught and demonstrated by Christ Jesus at our Reading Room. The Reading Room features the Bible and writings by Mary Baker Eddy that can be read, borrowed, or purchased. Anyone may stop by to study, pray or read and purchase issues of Christian Science periodicals including The Christian Science Monitor, an international newspaper.  Many Reading Rooms, including ours, also offer online resources available through the Mother Church in Boston, Massachusetts. If you’re interested in becoming a Reading Room attendant, reach out to Joanne at <a href="mailto:jolehner5@gmail.com">jolehner5@gmail.com</a>.
